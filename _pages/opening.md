## Undergraduate Studentship
Our research group specializes in understanding and predicting material properties and behaviors through atomistic simulations grounded in fundamental physics principles. We welcome inquiries from undergraduate students interested in physics, chemistry, or materials science (no formal prerequisites required).

## Master Studentship
We invite applications from Master's candidates possessing foundational knowledge in physics, chemistry, or materials science to undertake cutting-edge investigations in phonon-mediated material phenomena. Successful candidates will pursue theoretical studies focusing on:
(1) Phonon/Ionic /Electron transport
(2) Anharmonic lattice dynamics
(3) Solid-solid phase transition

## Postdoctoral Fellowship
Postdoctoral candidates with relevant expertise are invited to submit inquiries to Dr. Wang, including a CV, as one position is currently available.
